Proudly Made in San Diego, CA

Blake’s Stakes started in 2019 when I was only 13 years old after I made my mom a custom garden marker for Mother’s Day. My mom loved it so much and when our neighbor saw it, she placed a custom order immediately. One order leads to another, and another and another, which is when I had the idea to start mass-producing them. The original traditional garden markers sold well, but when I transitioned to making inspirational markers, the large orders started rolling in. Each indoor/outdoor ceramic garden marker is handmade and unique. Blake’s Stakes are fired to cone 5 (2118°) making them durable and designed to withstand the elements. They are available in a variety of sizes and colors to showcase your space. Thanks for your support and letting us be a part of your home!
